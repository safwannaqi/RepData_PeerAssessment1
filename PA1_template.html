####Strategy for imputing missing data:
Imputing all of the missing values in the dataset: I believe that the movement activity, especially walking, of any individual is highly dependant of the time of the day. We usually don't walk at night as most of us are asleep, and we immediately start walking around after waking up in the morning to go to work, school etc. Hence I have decided to impute my data for each interval with the mean value of that interval across all other dates.

imputed_data <- actmondata
missing_values <- is.na(imputed_data$steps)
imputed_data[missing_values, "steps"] <- 
    avesteps_per_int[as.character(imputed_data[missing_values, "interval"])]
